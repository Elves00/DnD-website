Session 1: Oh Captain my Captain

The sailboat arrives in a small coastal cave in a smuggling den. There's a storm a-brewing.
and our scoundrals are looking for a place to ride the night out. The group is carraying glass magic containment units for the local lord Drazmire
Running the location is Ekuza Baker upon arrival they find a lively port inside a cave mouth. There are three main cabins two on next to the docking area and one on the far side of the came home to. Francis Drake and Jim Rogers semi who are semi permanant residents.
The group is intially greeted by the pair and here the story begins.

Upon arrival the group can see about 14 people milling about the cave. A black sloop currently loading up spices is the only other boat in the port and is owned by Francis Drake. There is a small shindig happening in the middle. Our group a Tortle two Tabaxi rouge and a druid human begin their adventure starting with the brothers stealing a health potion and some coins with a little magical assistance while our druid almost gets robbed by some people who spiked his drink. After retreating to their quarters for the night a bunch of undead Lizard folk sneaks into the cave led by a necromancer of some description. A stray shout wakes the two brothers, and they investigate running into one of the lizards and raising the alarm by way of combat. A fight ensues with one of the partygoers dying but everyone else escapes onto the two boats after Francis Drake shouts thereafter the item. The group leaves the cave having almost killed the necromancer and with 3 extra crew on board. They sail through the night and most of the morning before arriving at a small fishing village. They inform the local shipmaster of events before the session ends.

Session 2: Just here for a drink......

After fleeing the cave the group continues on their journey before realizing they still need to resupply their ship. With this in mind, they head to the nearby small fishing village. Upon arrival, they are met by an old slightly large man in charge of the docks. The party notifies them of what happened at Tabalath Cove the man was thankful and upon hearing the mention of Lord Drazmire he gives them a port birth. The group after docking heads off to resupply. Oogway enters the first bar he can find inside he finds a couple of patrons who he sells a spell to called magic hand for 10 silver. He then finds a stream to go and get water from. While Oogway sells this spell Luke and Boo Duke steal some moonshine from behind the bar luke attempts to go down to the back of the bar to look for more moonshine but requires a distraction. Boo tries to make a distraction by sneakily throwing a bottle however he was spotted and so a bar brawl begins within him in the midst tho he sneakily leaves. Using this confusion to his advantage Boo attempts to steal some money from the till. Unfortunately for him, a patron uninterested in the fight but very interested in buying another beer notices him stealing. As the man raises the alarm the mob turn and starts chasing him. Luke goes down to the cellar, finds a hidden room, and knocks out a man making drugs before stealing two crates of said drugs and two flagons of moonshine.
A little chase ensues with luke hiding the crates in the water before everyone regroups out of town to collect water. They collect the water and bring it back to town where the grand escape starts.
